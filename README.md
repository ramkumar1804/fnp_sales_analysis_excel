# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard using Microsoft Excel

## Overview

This project is an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard developed using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard analyzes sales data from Ferns and Petals (FNP) to uncover business insights related to revenue, customer spending, product performance, and sales trends.

The project was completed as part of my Data Analytics learning journey to strengthen my skills in Excel-based business intelligence and dashboard development.

## Business Problem

Ferns and Petals (FNP) wanted to analyze its sales performance across different occasions, product categories, cities, and time periods to understand customer behavior and improve business decisions.

---

## Objectives

- Calculate Total Revenue
- Analyze Monthly Sales Performance
- Identify Top Revenue-Generating Products
- Measure Average Customer Spending
- Evaluate Average Delivery Time
- Analyze Revenue by Occasion
- Compare Revenue by Product Category
- Identify Top Cities by Orders
- Study Customer Ordering Patterns

---

## Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards
- Interactive Slicers
- Revenue by Occasion
- Revenue by Category
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Hour
- Top 5 Products
- Top 10 Cities
